The only tip that matters

Stay for the final 30 minutes of your park session. That is when the Super Mega Raid happens. Mega Mewtwo X and Y with Counter + Psystrike, GO Fest 2026 Location Background, and Mega Level unlocked. Nothing else you do that day matters as much as this. Everything else - the spawns, the raids, the research - you can do during City Experience too. But the Super Mega Raid with Location Background Mewtwo only happens in those 30 minutes inside Faelledparken.
